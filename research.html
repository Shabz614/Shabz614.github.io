Research and Projects

Research Ethics and Integrity

PIRI is dedicated to promoting a culture of research ethics and integrity. Our initiatives include educational programs for students and educators, fostering a commitment to ethical research practices.

Ongoing Projects

  • Student Training Programme: Enhancing research integrity culture among undergraduate, graduate, and PhD students.
  • Teaching for Educators Programme: Improving educators' competencies to teach research ethics and integrity effectively.

Upcoming Project

  • Research ethics and integrity Systems Programme: Enhancing research integrity policy, structures, and governance systems in universities and research institutions.

Environment and Energy Transition

PIRI actively engages in collaborative research projects focusing on environmental sustainability and the transition to clean energy. Our aim is to contribute to global efforts addressing climate change.

Ongoing Projects

  • Excellence Lab - Environment and Circular Economy: Facilitating collaborative research projects among researchers in Africa and Europe.

Upcoming Project

  • Environmental Policy Assessment: Evaluating policies for their environmental impact.

Peace, Security, and Governance

PIRI's commitment to fostering peace is manifested through our Peace Labs. These labs empower young peacemakers worldwide to contribute to a more peaceful world.

Ongoing Projects

  • International Summer Peace Labs: Forming young peacemakers from different countries, providing a platform for them to contribute to global peace.

Upcoming Project

  • Peacebuilding Initiatives: Exploring new avenues for sustainable peace through youth engagement.

Consultancy Services

At PIRI, we extend our research expertise to external organizations through our consultancy services. Leveraging the collective knowledge of our multidisciplinary network, we provide valuable insights, analysis, and solutions to address the unique challenges faced by organizations.

Research Expertise

Our consultancy services draw on PIRI's proficiency in the following key areas:

Research Ethics and Integrity

  • Guidance on establishing and enhancing research integrity policies.
  • Training programs for organizations aiming to cultivate a culture of ethical research.

Environment and Energy Transition

  • Sustainability assessments and recommendations for businesses and policymakers.
  • Expertise in environmental policy evaluation and development.

Peace, Security, and Governance

  • Consultation on peacebuilding initiatives and strategies..
  • Youth engagement programs for organizations focusing on peace and security.

Guidelines for Seeking Consultancy Support

  • Contact Us
  • Initial Discussion
  • Proposal Development
  • Agreement and Collaboration
  • Execution and Reporting
  • Feedback and Follow-up

Contact Us

Organizations interested in our consultancy services can initiate contact through our official communication channels.

Initial Discussion

A preliminary discussion will be arranged to understand the specific requirements and objectives of the organization.

Proposal Development

Based on the discussion, we will develop a detailed proposal outlining the scope of the consultancy, methodologies, timelines, and associated costs.

Agreement and Collaboration

Upon mutual agreement, we formalize the collaboration through a consultancy agreement, specifying roles, responsibilities, and deliverables.

Execution and Reporting

Our team, in collaboration with relevant experts, will execute the consultancy, providing regular updates and a comprehensive final report.

Execution and Reporting

We value feedback and encourage organizations to provide insights that contribute to the refinement of our services.

PIRI is committed to delivering high-quality consultancy services that align with the unique needs of our partners. For inquiries or to discuss potential collaborations, please contact us through the provided channels.
